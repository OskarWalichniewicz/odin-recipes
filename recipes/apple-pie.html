Apple Pie

Description

This is my mother's apple pie. This is the recipe I use all the time and I love it.

I also use a dash of nutmeg so if you wish you can put that in too.

Ingredients

  • 6 cups thinly sliced apples
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 recipe pastry for a 9-inch double-crust pie
